Who is Kit Loon Poh? Chef's Biography
The Bakery aspires to be more than simply the finest bakery. We hold ourselves to elevated standards of quality in our foods to ensure that each item we offer is a delight to both the eye and the palate. We strive for consistency so that each visit to our bakery delivers the same reliably satisfying experience while offering an exciting variety of products. Our products change to showcase seasonal ingredients and the exceptional creative talents of our chefs.
Born in Mauritius, Jenisha S. cummulates over 15 years of culinary experience. Since 2016 until this day, she occupied the position of Executive Chef at Kit Loon Poh.
